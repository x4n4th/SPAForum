About Us and Our Forums

Welcome to the Penn State Abington IST 420 Offical SPA Forums

These forums are made using a single page application.

No longer will forum users be required to leap through multiple different pages to post, read, and particpate in their interactive community.

For this project, we focused on using BootStrap CSS, AnjularJS, JSON, Entity Framework and SQL Server.

Our forums include a registration and user sign in to customize individual experiences

Users will be able to keep track of their activity in their page which includes a profile picture, post count, and current activity

If you have any question or comments feel free to email us and we will get back to you as soon as possible
